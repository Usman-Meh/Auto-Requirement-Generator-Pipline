Learn to appreciate the complexity of the global business environment and put your business skills to work anywhere in the world. Combining theory and application, gain in-depth knowledge about working around the world as part of the global economy. The program focuses on global trade and supply chain strategy, cross-cultural management, international marketing, finance, trade management and foreign market entry strategies.
