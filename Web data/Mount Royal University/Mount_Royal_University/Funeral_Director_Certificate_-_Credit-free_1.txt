Introduction to Funeral Service
— Get an overview of the funeral service profession and see if this is the career for you. This course also satisfies the entrance requirement for admission to the three funeral service education programs.
Mount Royal offers three programs under Funeral Service Education. Explore the three options, compare licensing requirements, costs, curriculum and courses and choose the funeral service program that’s right for you.
Funeral Service Diploma
Prepare to license as a funeral director and embalmer
Complete within a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 6 years
Study full-time or part-time
Diploma is the preferred credential with many funeral homes
Funeral Director Certificate
Concentrate on funeral directing only
Complete within a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 6 years
Study part-time
Embalmer Certificate
Specialize in embalming and restorative arts
Complete within a minimum of 1 year to a maximum of 6 years
Study part-time
The three Funeral Service Education programs integrate online theory and hands-on experiential learning, through the completion of supervised practica and work experience. In most cases, these can be completed in the community of your choice.
Students residing outside of Alberta — check with your Provincial Regulatory Board for guidelines and regulations on completing the practical and work experience courses within your province.
