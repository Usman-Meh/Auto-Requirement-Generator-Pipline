Advanced Accounting, Post Bachelor's Certificate
The Business Administration — Advanced Accounting, Post Bachelor's Certificate is designed for individuals with previous work experience who wish to increase their business knowledge or who are seeking a career change. It includes a core group of courses providing a general business background and a variety of options to suit your interests and needs. The certificate provides a theoretical and practical insight into the field of accounting so you can gain a current and employer-valued background.
Career possibilities
The Business Administration — Advanced Accounting, Post Bachelor's Certificate is intended to supplement your present career or allow you to change careers into or within the accounting profession.  It also serves as a bridging program for degree holders who need to complete prerequisite courses for entry into the
CPA
accounting designation program.
