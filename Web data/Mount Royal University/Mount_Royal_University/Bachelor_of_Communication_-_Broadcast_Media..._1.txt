Listen to our in-house radio station, CMRU.CA
Develop your technical skills and theoretical understanding to produce professional and engaging audio and video content for a variety of audiences. Take advantage of state-of-the-art video and audio production facilities and an online radio station while learning to write and produce commercials, news, documentaries, podcasts, advertising, short films, corporate videos and reality television.
