Check out the full Academic Upgrading program guide
Getting into the program of your choice can be competitive. Improve your high school grades or pick up courses you need for admission by taking academic upgrading in English, Math, Biology, Chemistry, Science, Social Science and Physics. Our courses cover the approved Alberta high school curriculum as well as additional skills that prepare students for first-year university coursework and can be used for admission to Mount Royal University and many other post-secondary institutions in Alberta.
Courses are delivered in-person and online.
