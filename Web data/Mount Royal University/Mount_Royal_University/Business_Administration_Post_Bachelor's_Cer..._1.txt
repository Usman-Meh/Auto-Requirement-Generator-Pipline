Human Resources, Post Bachelor's Certificate
The Business Administration - Human Resources, Post Bachelor's Certificate is designed for individuals who wish to increase their business knowledge or who are seeking a career change. You will take a core group of courses providing a general business background and can then take a variety of options to suit your interests and needs. The certificate will give you both theoretical and practical insight into the field of human resources and you will gain a current and employer-valued background.
Career possibilities
The Business Administration - Human Resources, Post Bachelor's Certificate is designed for individuals who already have work experience. It will be of value to human resource professionals who want to expand their knowledge of the field, individuals contemplating a career change to the human resources field and line managers, supervisors and administrators who implement human resource policies and programs.
