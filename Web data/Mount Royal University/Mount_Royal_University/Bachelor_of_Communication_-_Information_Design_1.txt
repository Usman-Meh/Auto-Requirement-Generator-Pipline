Watch an Open House info session about the Bachelor of Communication Studies
Information designers carefully plan the structure, context and presentation of data and information in an effort to make it appropriate, useful and engaging. Learn to prioritize effectiveness and function by centering the needs, wants, experiences, circumstances and constraints of specific, well-understood communities of users. Br ready to work in a variety of sectors while developing information products that are clear, concise and visually engaging for online, print, mobile and augmented and virtual reality environments.
Build your skills in creative problem solving, innovation, visual communication, teamwork, functional writing, research and data visualization.
