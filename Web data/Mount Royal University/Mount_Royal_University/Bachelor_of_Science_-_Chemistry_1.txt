Chemistry is the science that connects everything, everywhere. Gain a deeper understanding of the molecular processes behind the machinery of life. Get involved in experiential learning, spend time learning in the laboratory and see how science is practiced in the community. Study the breadth of chemistry with the general chemistry option or explore a mix of chemical analysis, biology and chemistry with the analytical biochemistry concentration.
