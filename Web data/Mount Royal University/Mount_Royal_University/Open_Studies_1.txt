Want to start post-secondary studies but don’t know where to begin? Maybe you're missing high school course requirements for admission to your intended program or need to upgrade your marks. Open Studies may be the answer. Open Studies gives you the freedom to find the program that’s right for you while earning Mount Royal University credits. Sampling a few credit courses can help you decide on the program that’s right for you. Best of all, while you’re checking things out, you earn credits toward your future Mount Royal University credential.
Courses are delivered in-person and online.
