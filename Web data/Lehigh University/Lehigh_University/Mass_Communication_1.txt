Mass Communication Minor Overview
Lehighâ€™s interdisciplinary undergraduate minor in mass communication is offered for students interested in developing communication skills and a better understanding of how people share meaning through persuasive use of rhetoric, logic and symbols in public, one-to-one and small group communication.
Mass Communication Careers
The skills students develop with a minor in mass communication can enhance any career that requires communication with individuals or groups of people. Graduates can pursue careers in journalism, broadcasting, public relations, advertising and other fields.
