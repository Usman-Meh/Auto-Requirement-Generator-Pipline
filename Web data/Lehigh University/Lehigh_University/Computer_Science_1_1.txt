Ph.D. in Computer Science Overview
Lehigh’s graduate programs in computer science provide a balance between formal classroom instruction and research and are tailored to the individual student’s professional goals. The programs appeal to individuals with backgrounds in electrical or computer engineering, computer or information science, mathematics, or the physical sciences.
