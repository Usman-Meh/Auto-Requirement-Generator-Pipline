FLEX MBA and Engineering, MBAE Overview
Designed for engineers hoping to advance into leadership roles, the FLEX MBA in engineering prepares graduates for leadership positions in industrial planning, venture capital, and engineering management; and as senior managers in roles requiring both technical and business acumen.
The program is designed for students with an undergraduate degree in engineering, and classes can be taken both on campus and online. It offers engineering concentrations in chemical engineering, civil engineering, electrical engineering, industrial and systems engineering, manufacturing systems engineering, materials science engineering, and mechanical engineering.
