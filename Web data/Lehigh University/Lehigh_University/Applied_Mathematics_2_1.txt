Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics Overview
Students in the Ph.D. program take the qualifying examination in the same two areas, algebra and real analysis, within two years. They test in statistics and applied probability for those in the statistics track, and analysis and differential equations for students in the applied mathematics track.
