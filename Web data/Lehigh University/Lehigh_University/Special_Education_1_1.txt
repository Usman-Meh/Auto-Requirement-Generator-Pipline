Master’s in Special Education Overview
Lehigh’s master’s degree in Special Education is designed for the college graduate who holds instructional certification in special education and is interested in pursuing advanced studies in special education. The mission of the 10-course (30-credit) program is to enhance the skills of highly qualified special educators who are not only skilled practitioners, but also leaders in establishing effective teaching and learning environments for individuals with disabilities in their schools and communities.
