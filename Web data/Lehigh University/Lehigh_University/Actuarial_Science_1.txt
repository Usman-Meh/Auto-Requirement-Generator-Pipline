What is Actuarial Science?
Lehighâ€™s undergraduate minor in actuarial science teaches students the discipline that applies mathematical and statistical methods to assess risk in insurance, finance, and other industries and professions.
The program offers small class sizes, allowing students to work closely with both their professors and fellow students.
Actuarial Science Career Path
Lehigh alumni with minors in actuarial science work as actuaries, are employed in the financial industry, teach in high school or college, and work for technology companies.
