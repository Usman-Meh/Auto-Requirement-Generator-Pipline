Museum Studies Minor Overview
Lehigh’s undergraduate minor in museum studies educates students in research, collections management and exhibition planning; education, communication and exhibition design; and special topics in museum and curatorial studies.
Museum Studies Career Opportunities
Lehigh alumni with a minor in museum studies work at museums—large and small—around the world.
