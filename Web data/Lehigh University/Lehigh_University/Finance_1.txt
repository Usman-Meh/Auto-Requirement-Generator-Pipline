Finance Major
Finance Major Overview
Lehigh’s undergraduate major in finance builds on the College of Business’ integrated core. The curriculum focuses on the private sector involving corporations and financial institutions. Students may choose from a wide range of finance electives. They are encouraged to take courses in related areas such as accounting, computers, economics and quantitative methods.
Read more about the College of Business core curriculum >
Degrees Offered
Bachelor of Science in Business and Economics degree with a major in Finance
Bachelor of Science in Integrated Business and Engineering degree with a major in Finance
What Can You Do with a Finance Major?
Lehigh’s finance alumni work in real estate, in corporate finance, in budgeting and risk control, in portfolio management, securities analysis, and in financial institutions as investment bankers and lenders.
