Master’s in Biological Chemical Engineering
Master’s in Biological Chemical Engineering Overview
Biological chemical engineering is a quickly-growing field. The Master of Engineering Degree Program provides graduates with the flexibility to adapt to a variety of industrial experiences and forms the base for an expanding career. Graduates from this program will have the skills to identify, evaluate, and manage cost and performance decisions throughout the major stages of life in large-scale developments in the pharmaceutical and related industries.
The 30-credit hour program is comprised of 12 credit hours of core chemical and biotechnology courses, 6 hours of elective chemical engineering courses, 6 hours of biology elective courses, and 6 hours of chemistry elective courses.
