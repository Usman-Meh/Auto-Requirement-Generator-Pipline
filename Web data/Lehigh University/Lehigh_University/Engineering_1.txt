Engineering Minor Overview
Lehigh’s undergraduate minor in engineering provides insight to students in Lehigh’s College of Business or the College of Arts and Sciences into what engineers do and how they think, and an understanding of the tools and techniques they use to innovate and solve problems.
Career Opportunities
Lehigh alumni with a minor in engineering will enter the workforce with a broader understanding of the technical world, allowing them to compete and innovate at a time of rapid technological advance.
