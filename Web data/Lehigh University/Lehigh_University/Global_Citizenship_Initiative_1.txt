Global Citizenship Initiative Certificate Overview
The Global Citizenship Initiative is an interdisciplinary certificate that structures educational experiences which enable students to negotiate international boundaries and develop their own sense of personal, social and corporate responsibility to the global community as an engineer, a businessperson, artist or a social or natural scientist.
