Bioengineering Master's Degree Overview
Addressing the modern challenges of healthcare requires innovators who combine life and physical sciences with engineering to diagnose and treat disease more effectively and affordably. Lehigh’s bioengineering graduate program trains students to work across these disciplines—offering diverse opportunities for advanced studies and research. Students combine education in life and physical sciences and engineering to develop effective and affordable solutions for healthcare and biotechnology problems.
