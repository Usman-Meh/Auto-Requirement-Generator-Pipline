Orton Gillingham Teacher Graduate Certificate
This program is accredited at the IMSLEC Teaching Level. The International Multisensory Structured Language Education Council (IMSLEC) provides national accreditation to programs that prepare specialists in multisensory structured language education. The program is also one of only 18 universities awarded Accreditation PLUS by the International Dyslexia Association.
