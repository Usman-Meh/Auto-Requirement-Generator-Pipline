Data Science Minor (Business)
The department of mathematics and computer science of Maxwell Becton College of Arts and Sciences, and the department of finance, decision sciences, economics, and international business of the Silberman College of Business offer data science minors at the Florham Campus, Madison, New Jersey. This is the business data sciences minor. Current students should refer to their degree audit in Self-Service for the most accurate program requirements.
