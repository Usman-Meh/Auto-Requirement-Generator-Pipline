Computer Programming Undergraduate Certificate
The computer programming undergraduate certificate provides students with broad-based expertise in computer programming languages, including Python, Java and C++. There are more than 200 contact hours of class instruction.
This 15-credit certificate is offered by the Lee Gildart and Oswald Haase School of Computer Sciences and Engineering at the Metropolitan Campus, Teaneck, New Jersey.
Prerequisites
A high school diploma
Curriculum Plan
15 credits (5 courses) to be chosen from the following. To take any course in the certificate, the student must meet all the pre-requisites for the course.
CSCI1105
Survey of Computers and Software
CSCI1201
Computer Programming I
CSCI1202
