Information Technology Undergraduate Certificate
The courses for the 15 credit Certificate in Information Technology prepare students to apply the principles of computer networking, programming, and multi-media to an office, business or industry environment. It can help students assume responsibilities involving IT system planning, installation and maintenance in offices.
The certificate is offered by the Lee Gildart and Oswald Haase School of Computer Sciences and Engineering at the Metropolitan Campus, Teaneck, New Jersey.
To take any course in this certificate program, a student must meet all the prerequisites for that course.
