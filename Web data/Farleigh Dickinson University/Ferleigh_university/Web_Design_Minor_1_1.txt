Web Design Minor
In this 15-credit minor students will explore the field of web design, learning the industry standard tools and software for the development of personal and commercial websites for the computer and mobile devices. A focus is on design, composition, color therapy, typography, interaction, and responsive design.
Required Courses (9 credits)
CGD 1313
Creative imagery with Photoshop
CGD 3303
Web Design
CGD 3315
Word Press, Wix, and Widgets
Elective Courses (6 credits)
Select two of the following courses:
CGD 2810
Ui/UX Design
CGD 3305
Dynamic Design
CGD 3377
Web Design II
