Computer Science K-12 Teacher Certification Minor
The 15-credit computer science K–12 teacher certification minor prepares QUEST students to teach computer science in grades K–12 and to add a computer science certification as a second endorsement.
The minor is offered by the department of mathematics and computer science, Maxwell Becton College of Arts and Sciences, at the Florham Campus, Madison, New Jersey.
Requirements
Required Courses (12 credits)
CSCI1145
Computer Science Fundamentals
CSCI1204
Computer Programming Using C#
CSCI2104
Computer Programming Using Python
CSCI2244
Computational Thinking
Elective (3 credits)
Choose one from the following courses:
CSCI1109
Computer Ethics
CSCI2100
Mobile App Development
CSCI2205

CSCI3294

CSCI3388
Introduction to Game Development
CSCI3389

CSCI3392
