Data Science Minor (Computer Science)
The department of mathematics and computer science of Maxwell Becton College of Arts and Sciences, and the department of marketing, information systems and decision sciences of the Silberman College of Business offer data science minors at the Florham Campus, Madison, New Jersey. This is the mathematics and computer science data sciences minor.
