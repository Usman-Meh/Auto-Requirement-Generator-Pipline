Leadership Theory and Practice Graduate Certificate
This 18-credit, 6-course certificate is designed for individuals who would like to increase their leadership skills. There are differences between managers and leaders, and this course of study looks at the attributes of leaders, different theories that can be used, and how leaders can vary their style based on the situation at hand. These credits can also be applied to the
Master of Administrative Science
degree program.
