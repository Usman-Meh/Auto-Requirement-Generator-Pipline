Orton Gillingham Therapist Graduate Certificate
Orton Gillingham Therapist Certificate (39 credits) program is accredited at the IMSLEC Therapy Level. The International Multisensory Structured Language Education Council (IMSLEC) provides national accreditation to programs that prepare specialists in multisensory structured language education.
