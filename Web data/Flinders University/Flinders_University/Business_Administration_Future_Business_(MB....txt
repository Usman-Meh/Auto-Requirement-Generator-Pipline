Master of Business Administration Future Business
Overview
The Master of Business Administration Future Business provides a comprehensive foundation covering core business and management disciplines.
This course is the fastest way to complete an MBA. In only 12 months, and with three study periods to choose from, you set the pace. You’ll graduate with skills that could see you embracing leadership roles in organisations including the private sector, government departments, not for profits, international organisations, social enterprises and more.
Real industry experience is a major feature of this fast-tracked course, with up to 26 weeks of experience available through Work Integrated Learning and industry programs. You’ll undertake an industry placement or choose a specialised industry entrepreneurial program, such as the Defence Entrepreneurship Program (DEP). Flinders is the first university in South Australia to offer the DEP, a landmark program that will see you learn how to use modern innovation tools and techniques to address national security and intelligence issues, and help you create international defence entrepreneurial networks.
Other opportunities include industry programs such as the Wine and Tourism Entrepreneurship Program or the Advanced Manufacturing Entrepreneurship Program.

Your career
Take charge of your global business career, learn how to make waves in the industry with this 12-month course. The MBA Future Business prepares you for a wide range of career opportunities in areas including management consulting, business acquisition, strategy and leadership.
Potential occupations include:
chief executive officer
managing director
chief operating officer
executive director
national director
chief robotics officer
artificial intelligence architect
in-world marketing architect
loyalty engineer
program director
marketing manager
machine and people ethics manager
senior business development manager.
Potential employers include:
multinational corporations
large private companies
public sector
market research consultancies
corporate agencies
not-for-profit organisations.
What you will study
The MBA contains 12 topics, you can also choose an industry placement or industry entrepreneurial program instead of one core topic. Opportunities include Wine and Tourism Entrepreneurship Program and the Advanced Manufacturing Entrepreneurship Program.
You will also undertake a major industry project which will bring together everything you have learnt over the course and put it into practice.

Entry requirements
Graduate Certificate
Applicants must either:
hold a degree or equivalent qualification from an approved tertiary institution OR
have at least one years’ experience in a related context or two years’ experience in a non-related field.
Graduate Diploma
Applicants must either:
hold a degree or equivalent qualification from an approved tertiary institution OR
hold the Flinders University Graduate Certificate in Business Administration.
Master
Applicants must normally hold a Bachelor Honours degree or equivalent qualification OR hold a Bachelor degree in a related field or equivalent qualification OR have completed a Graduate Diploma in Business Administration or a Graduate Certificate in Business Administration, or equivalent qualification.
