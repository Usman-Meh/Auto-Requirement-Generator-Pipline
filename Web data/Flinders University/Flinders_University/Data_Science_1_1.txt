Data Science
Overview
If you are a graduate with a previous degree in information technology or computer science but are looking to upgrade and extend your knowledge and skills in this dramatically changing area, the Flinders Master of Data Science provides the perfect opportunity.
Continuing advances in technology and the growing complexity of big data is leading us towards a world where an incredible amount of information is at our fingertips. It’s also creating a need for specialists who can make sense of, and find new insights from all that information to create innovative solutions.
Flinders Master of Data Science will give you the skills to analyse complex data, form powerful insights and communicate new ideas to help solve the myriad challenges faced by government and industry in the 21
st
century.

Flinders Master of Data Science is taught by skilled professionals with a deep understanding of the important role that data science plays in harnessing the power of big data. Your lecturers and supervisors are practising data scientists who understand the current and future needs of industry and have the practical skills and industry networks to ensure that you graduate more than ready for the next step in your career path.
Your career
Take charge of your future and position yourself as a specialist fully prepared to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Flinders Master of Data Science is a globally relevant, industry-linked degree that will give you the skills to pursue a high-level career in a wide range of industry sectors.
Potential occupations include:
data scientist
data modeller
data analyst
data engineer
machine learning/deep learning specialist
marketing analyst.
Potential employers include:
telecommunications organisations
aerospace and defence
government
higher education institutions
private organisations
start-ups.
Professional accreditation/recognition:
The course is accredited by the Australian Computer Society at the level of professional. The course accreditation is recognised internationally for entry to professional practice by seven other accrediting bodies through the Seoul Accord.
What you will study
Flinders postgraduate Data Science program has been designed in close consultation with industry. You’ll develop in-demand skills to help solve the challenges of today and tomorrow. You’ll also learn soft skills that will help you navigate the world by working with multiple disciplines and cultures. You’ll hone your leadership and problem-solving skills, and develop interpersonal skills that will put you ahead of the competition.
Practical experience
The Master of Data Science involves significant project work, notably a major individual project that may tackle an industry-relevant problem or a research question.
