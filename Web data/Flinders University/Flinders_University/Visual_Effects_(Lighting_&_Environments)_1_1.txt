Visual Effects (Lighting & Environments)
Overview
In this unique partnership between Flinders University and CDW Studios you’ll gain the skills to excel in the growing world of visual effects lighting and environments.
Over 12 weeks, you’ll learn from world-leading industry experts who are at the top of their field and have worked on some of the most famous and intensive VFX projects ever made. This specialised course will sharpen your VFX skills by deepening your understanding of the 3DDMP pipeline and setting you up with a generalist skillset including workflow techniques and delivery expectations. You’ll finish the course ready to enter the workforce as a specialist.
This course is offered in Adelaide, Sydney and Melbourne. Some topics may be offered online.

Your career
A career in visual effects lighting and environments could lead to a range of careers locally, nationally and internationally.
In this degree, you’ll build a world-class portfolio and work alongside industry to grow your professional networks from day one.
Potential occupations could include

What you will study
Graduate Certificate in Visual Effects (Lighting & Environments)
This 12 week certificate is a specialised, high-quality program in which students will enhance their creative and practical skills in lighting and environments. A typical study program may include:
Skills acquisition project delivered by CDW studios. This project will involve specialised training in developing lighting and environment assets from concept to completion.
Practical experience
Practical experience is embedded into this program with students learning with and from industry experts at Flinders and CDW Animation.
