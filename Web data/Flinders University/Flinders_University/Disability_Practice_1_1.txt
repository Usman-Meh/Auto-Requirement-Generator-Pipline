Why study postgraduate Disability Practice?
Develop specialised knowledge and become NDIS-aware, allowing you to support the inclusion and citizenship of people with disability in their local community.
Our programs are taught on the strong principles of human rights, equity, justice, and access, providing a unique education that aligns to the needs of people with disability, industry, and the community.
Our teaching staff are experienced Disability and Community Inclusion professionals, academics, and researchers with rich and diverse backgrounds. Learn from experts in the field who have a broad range of connections to industry.
The Graduate Certificate can be completed in as little as six months, allowing to be job-ready, faster.
You can choose to continue studying with our Masters of Disability Practice and Leadership, strengthening your leadership capabilities.
Study anywhere in Australia, with all topics available 100% online, allowing you to balance your work, life, and study commitments
Your career
This degree will allow you to specialise in disability practice in a wide range of careers in allied health, education, community and human services.
Potential occupations include:
Program coordinator
Skilled disability practitioner
Case manager
Team leader
Human services disability specialist.

Core topics in disability theory and practice and disability, rights, and embodiment.
