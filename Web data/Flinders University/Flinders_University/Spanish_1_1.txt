Spanish
Choose a major in Spanish
EL mundo habla español -
the world speaks Spanish.
Study a major in Spanish to gain comprehensive skills to understand, speak, read, and write Spanish proficiently. You'll explore diverse usages and cultural expressions of Spanish across the Spanish-speaking world, through a wide range of real-life materials incorporated into classes and study resources.
Expect to be truly immersed: you will deepen your appreciation and understanding of Hispanic cultures and societies, while exploring the rich linguistic and cultural diversity of Spanish-speaking regions globally. You will be equipped to effectively communicate and engage with the Hispanic world across various professional and personal contexts.
The Spanish major is available across two streams:
Beginners (for students with no knowledge of the Spanish language)
Intermediate (for students with SACE, IB or equivalent proficiency in Spanish)
Why study Spanish?
Spanish is the second most spoken native language in the world, with over 500 million speakers across 21 countries
Increase your employment opportunities and earning potential
Learning Spanish makes learning subsequent languages easier, due to exposure to new language patterns - especially other Romance languages like French, Italian or Portuguese
Enhance understanding of your own language, as many English words have Latin roots shared with Spanish
Learning Spanish provides access to its rich literature, music, films, and other cultural works in their original form
Experience a diverse curriculum.
Your career
This major opens up a wide array of career paths.
Spanish proficiency opens up a wide array of career paths across sectors like business, healthcare, legal, education, media, and public service by enabling communication with the large global Spanish-speaking population.
Knowledge of Spanish also provides a a competitive economic advantage for individuals through higher earnings and job prospects, and for businesses by facilitating trade, investment, and access to the large - and growing - Hispanic market.

How many units?
This major caters for Beginners and Intermediate (post-secondary) students. It is comprised of 36 units, including:
Two Year 1 topics (9 units)
Two Year 2 topics (9 units)
Two Year 3 topics (9 units)
Plus two additional topics (9 units) from the Year 2 or Year 3 topics.
Modern Greek can also be taken as a minor for Beginners and Intermediate (post-secondary) students. Your 18 units will include:
Two Year 1 topics (9 units)
Two Year 2 topics (9 units).
