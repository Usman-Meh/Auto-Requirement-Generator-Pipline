Virtual Production
Undertake a Master of Virtual Production
Future-proof your career in the screen, VR and VFX industries
Welcome to one of the first and only postgraduate degrees in Virtual Production in Australia*. This degree covers virtual production, motion capture, virtual reality and visual effects.
You'll study with practising virtual production specialists who have strong links to international industry partners. You'll also study in Flinders' purpose-built motion capture studio,
The Void
, making this masters degree a game-changer for the film and television industries.
Endless possibilities have opened up with the introduction of virtual reality and virtual production to the filmmaking business – think Avatar, The Mandalorian or Game of Thrones.
Video is key. Stay ahead of the game and future-proof your career by developing new skills in virtual production.
*Correct as of August 2022.

Why study Virtual Production (postgraduate) at Flinders?
Australia’s only course embedded in a virtual production studio
One of the only postgraduate degrees in Virtual Production in Australia
Use the same technology as Disney in Flinders’ motion capture studio, the Void
Strong industry connections with international partners such as Technicolor and Motion Picture Company, Facewear Tech, Unreal Engine and Fika Entertainment
World #15 Visual Effects School (The Rookies Awards 2022)
Practice-based courses teaching up-to-date industry skills
Growing demand locally and internationally
We have strong connections to industry and long experience in preparing graduates for rewarding, meaningful careers.
