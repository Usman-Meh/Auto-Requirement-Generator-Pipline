Visual Effects (3D Assets)
Overview
In this unique partnership between Flinders University and CDW Studios you’ll gain the skills to excel in the fast-paced world of visual effects and 3D.
Over 12 weeks, you’ll learn from world-leading industry experts who are at the top of their field and have worked on some of the most famous and intensive VFX projects ever made. This specialised course will sharpen your 3D asset modelling skills by deepening your understanding of the artistic fundamentals of modelling and sculpting, Maya, poly count, mesh flow, and muscle movement.
This course is offered in Adelaide, Sydney and Melbourne. Some topics may be offered online.
This graduate certificate can be used as a pathway into the

What you will study
Graduate Certificate in Visual Effects (3D Assets)
This 12 week certificate is a specialised, high-quality program in which students will enhance their creative and practical skills in 3D and visual effects. A typical study program may include:
Skills acquisition project delivered by CDW studios. This project will involve specialised training in developing 3D ASSETS from concept to completion.
Practical experience
Practical experience is embedded into this program with students learning with and from industry experts at Flinders and CDW Animation.

Connect with industry leaders
You will benefit from our strong connections to industry leaders that will provide you with practical, real-world industry experience through placements.
The unique partnership between Flinders and CDW allows our Creative Arts students to work directly with industry to develop the specialist creative and technological skills to enter the growing VFX industry.
Entry requirements
Graduate Certificate
Applicants must normally hold a bachelor degree or equivalent qualification in Animation or Visual Effects related disciplines.
Additionally, applicants will have to demonstrate their creative abilities through the submission of a portfolio of original work and CV.
