Why Study Dance?
Explore the artistic potential of movement
Our program fosters personal and artistic development through dance. We offer a range of opportunities for creative exploration, performance, and interdisciplinary inquiry.
As a student in the
Department of Visual and Performing Arts
’s dance minor, you’ll gain a technical foundation in modern and jazz techniques as well as an exposure to fundamental training in diverse dance forms.
The Dance Minor, offered through Clark’s Theatre Program, provides a multidisciplined perspective on the practice and study of contemporary performance.
Minimum number of courses to complete this minor: 6
View Course
Hands-On Learning
Students have the opportunity to perform and choreograph throughout the year with a number of groups including Dance Society, Variant Dance, and Hip Hop Collabo.
Careers
Dance education is an asset to those seeking careers in the performing arts, dance education, arts administration, and event planning.
