The master's program in Information Technology offers comprehensive training in the latest technologies and prepares students for a successful career in the IT industry. With a focus on practical applications and theory knowledge, the graduates are well equipped to master the challenges of the modern IT world.
Course objectives
:
Deepening specialist knowledge Expansion of the theoretical foundations: Imparting advanced knowledge in core areas of information technology such as Information and Coding Theory, Transmission Technology, Communication Networks, Digital Signal Processing, Image Processing, Real-Time Programming, Integrated Circuits, Robotics, Advanced Control Engineering, Embedded Systems, Embedded Security Updating technical know-how: Ensuring students are familiar with the latest technologies, tools and methods.
Development of practical skills Project-based learning approaches:
Conducting hands-on projects to solve real-world problems and develop real-world skills. Technological applications: Use of modern technologies and software in laboratories and projects to gain practical experience.
Promoting innovation and creativity Innovation projects:
encouraging students to develop innovative IT solutions and new approaches to problem solving.
Research and development: support in the implementation of research projects and the publication of research results.
Preparation for leadership roles Management skills:
imparting knowledge and skills in the areas of project management, IT strategy and business management. Teamwork and Communication: Promote teamwork, communication and presentation skills through group projects and seminars.
Global thinking and intercultural competence International perspective: providing a global understanding of the IT industry and its trends. Intercultural cooperation: promoting intercultural competence through international projects and exchange programs.
Research, develop and shape the future!
The master's program in Information Technology offers a solid foundation for a successful career in a dynamic and constantly growing industry. The combination of technical knowledge, practical skills and management skills enables graduates to be successful in a variety of roles and industries and contribute to the further development of the IT landscape.

On the Master's degree programme in Information Technology, you will learn the basics of information theory and coding and acquire knowledge about techniques and systems for transmitting data via various media. You will also learn how to process digital signals and how to programme systems that have to work in real time.
In the further course of your Master's degree programme, you will learn the fundamentals and applications of robotics as well as the safety aspects of embedded systems. As part of your internship, you can gain practical experience and apply your theoretical knowledge before writing your Master's thesis.
We will be happy to send you further information. You will also find the complete curriculum there.

As a state-recognised private university, SRH University is financed by tuition fees. The fees go towards equipping our campuses, providing services for our students, and ensuring quality assurance. This way, we ensure you receive the best possible education under optimal study conditions.
Tuition fees
EU/EEA citizenship, including Switzerland, Western Balkans and Ukraine: €770 per month
Non-EU/EEA citizenship: €6450 per semester
The monthly/semester tuition fees remain the same for the entire duration of the study programme. A one-off enrolment fee of €750 (EU/EEA) or €1000 (non-EU/EEA) is to be paid once.
Goal-oriented studies require good organisational skills and solid financial planning on how to pay rent, living costs, and tuition fees. Various financing options, such as educational loans or scholarship programmes, are available for students.
