The Master's programme in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at our SRH University focuses on preparing students with the necessary knowledge and skills to develop, implement and evaluate advanced AI technologies. The programme objectives focus on teaching theoretical knowledge, practical skills and innovative thinking to prepare students for a successful career in this dynamic and fast-growing field.
Expanding specialist knowledge in artificial intelligence
Fundamentals of AI
: teaching basic concepts such as machine learning, computer vision, DSP, image processing, deep neural networks.
Advanced AI techniques
: In-depth study of topics such as deep learning, reinforcement learning, unsupervised learning and explainable AI.
Development of practical skills
Programming and algorithms
: Mastery of programming languages and tools used in AI development, such as Python.
Project-based learning approaches
: Carrying out practical projects to develop and implement AI solutions in various application areas.
Promote innovation and creativity
Innovative applications
: Identifying and developing new use cases for AI in areas such as healthcare, finance, automotive, retail and the public sector.
Research and development
: promoting participation in research projects and the publication of research results on current challenges and trends in AI.
Preparation for leadership roles
Management skills
: Providing skills in project management, product development and IT strategy related to AI projects.
Entrepreneurial skills
: support in the development of business models and start-ups in the AI field.
Strengthening analytical and critical thinking skills
Problem-based learning
: fostering analytical thinking and problem-solving skills by analysing real AI use cases and challenges.
Data analysis and management
: teaching methods for efficiently managing, analysing and interpreting large amounts of data.
Promoting professional and ethical responsibility
Ethics and regulation
: Discussing ethical issues and regulatory challenges related to AI, such as data privacy, bias and the impact on jobs.
Sustainability and social responsibility
: Integrating sustainability and social responsibility aspects into the development and implementation of AI solutions.
Global thinking and intercultural competence
International Perspective
: Providing a global understanding of AI technology and its application in different countries and cultures.
Intercultural cooperation
: Promoting cooperation in international teams and projects to develop intercultural competences.
The demand for specialists in the field of artificial intelligence has risen significantly worldwide. Companies and organisations are increasingly looking for experts who can develop, implement and apply AI technologies. AI experts are finding employment opportunities in a variety of industries, including healthcare, finance, automotive, e-commerce, education, computer science and many others. AI is used in various applications such as robotics, computer vision, natural language processing (NLP), data science and machine learning. Due to the high demand for AI experts, competition for qualified professionals is strong.

Start your application now
At the beginning of your Master's degree programme in Information Technology - Artificial Intelligence, you will learn about the theories of information transmission and compression, among other things, and deal with basic principles and techniques for transmitting signals in wired and wireless networks. You will acquire in-depth knowledge in the fields of digital signal processing, image processing and machine learning.
In the further course of your Master's degree programme, you will deepen your knowledge in the field of artificial intelligence. In the Deep Neural Networks module, you will familiarise yourself with a powerful machine learning technique and expand your programming skills in Python Programming. As part of your internship, you can gain practical experience and apply your theoretical knowledge before writing your Master's thesis.
We will be happy to send you further information. You will also find the complete curriculum there.

As a state-recognised private university, SRH University is financed by tuition fees. The fees go towards equipping our campuses, providing services for our students, and ensuring quality assurance. This way, we ensure you receive the best possible education under optimal study conditions.
Tuition fees
EU/EEA citizenship, including Switzerland, Western Balkans and Ukraine: €770 per month
Non-EU/EEA citizenship: €6450 per semester
The monthly/semester tuition fees remain the same for the entire duration of the study programme. A one-off enrolment fee of €750 (EU/EEA) or €1000 (non-EU/EEA) is to be paid once.
Goal-oriented studies require good organisational skills and solid financial planning on how to pay rent, living costs, and tuition fees. Various financing options, such as educational loans or scholarship programmes, are available for students.
