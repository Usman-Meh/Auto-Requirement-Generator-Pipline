Why choose this programme
The programme provides theoretical and practical skills in the new information technologies, integrating scientific and engineering contents
It allows you to choose between specialisation in Science or Engineering
You will gain a broad background in design and development work and in research
The programme combines teaching with practical experience in the laboratory, often based on interesting concrete case studies
It focuses on the needs of the world of work, promoting development of theses involving practical applications in companies and research centres

Experiences abroad
In terms of experience abroad, the programme offers the opportunity to serve an internship with an international organisation or participate in an exchange programme. These experiences offer opportunities for direct contact with international organisations, enriching the student’s understanding of global dynamics and providing a real advantage in the international labour market.
