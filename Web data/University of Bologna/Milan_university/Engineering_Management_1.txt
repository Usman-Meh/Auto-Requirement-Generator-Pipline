Why choose this programme
The programme offers sound technological and economic-managerial skills to guide the development of product, process and business innovation
It provides in-depth knowledge of industrial technologies and digital transformation processes and of their economic and organisational implications.
It trains multidisciplinary, versatile professional profiles, highly sought after in the world of work (very high employment rate after graduation)
It combines in-depth classroom training with project work, workshops and work experiences through internships and in-company theses
The programme offers the opportunity for study periods abroad, thanks to a network of collaborations with prestigious international universities.
