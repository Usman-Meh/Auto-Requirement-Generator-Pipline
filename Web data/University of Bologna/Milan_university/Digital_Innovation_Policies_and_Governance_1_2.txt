Why choose this programme
Become a specialist in digital innovation for public administration.
Acquire effective multidisciplinary knowledge for tackling the digital transformation of public services and offices.
Develop skills facilitating organisational, technological and managerial changes in the digital transition of the public administration.
Learn to apply data science to assess the impact of decisions and innovations in political and administrative contexts.
Practice data analysis and understand how digital technologies operate in organisational contexts through laboratory work and internships while you prepare your thesis.
