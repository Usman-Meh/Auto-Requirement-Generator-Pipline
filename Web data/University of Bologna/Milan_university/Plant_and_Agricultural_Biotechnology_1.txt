Why choose this programme
Gain in-depth knowledge of the biology and physiology, genomics and bioinformatics of agricultural crops.
Develop expertise in economic processes for the exploitation of biotechnological innovation and the mechanisms regulating protection of intellectual property rights.
Learn to develop and implement biotechnological interventions for the selection, genetic improvement and protection of crops.
Participate in the management of basic and applied research projects involving technology transfer to industry.
Acquire transversal skills that will enable you to contribute to the debate on the comparison between different agricultural production systems.
