Why choose this programme
You will be able to understand and critically contextualise texts and contexts from classical antiquity
You will study or prepare your thesis or do your internship in prestigious foreign locations
You will come into contact with the scientific research world through seminars, conferences and internships
You will have access to a rich library heritage and state-of-the-art computer labs
You will acquire multidisciplinary and soft skills that will help you enter the world of work more easily

Experiences abroad
The programme offers you the opportunity to complete your education through an experience abroad: Erasmus+ and Overseas exchanges; internship opportunities abroad; the possibility of spending a period abroad, at one of the most prestigious institutes for classical philology, to prepare your thesis. These experiences will put you directly in contact with the international research world.
