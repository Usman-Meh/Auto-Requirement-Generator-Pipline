Why choose this programme
A programme that aims to train leaders of cultural organisations and creative industries worldwide.
Based on a strong interdisciplinary approach encompassing management studies, humanities and practical skills.
The ideal environment to develop teamwork skills and improve your entrepreneurial skills
The international dimension is reinforced by exchange possibilities and international guests.
Practical, research-based approach: a heterogeneous faculty with academic and professional expertise

Experiences abroad
The programme offers the opportunity to follow courses abroad by participating in exchange programmes with foreign universities; you will be able to do internships at international cultural and artistic organisations. These experiences enrich your understanding of global dynamics and provide a real advantage in the international labour market.
GIOCA stands out because it conveys with passion and curiosity what is happening in the world about managing and innovating in the field of culture, arts and creativity with the ability to analyze the local contexts. Furthermore, the heterogeneous group of professors and students who go along with you for two years will have a fundamental value.
Giulia Naldi, graduate 2017
