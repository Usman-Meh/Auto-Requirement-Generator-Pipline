Why choose this programme
For its distinctiveness, focusing on analysing social problems within the sociology of deviance and criminology.
For the quality of the training offered, with highly qualified tenured teachers in every subject.
For the focus on organising specialised seminars held by national and international professionals.
For the high percentage of graduates satisfied with their choice of programme.
To develop the ability to seek solutions to social problems related to criminal events and victimisation processes.
