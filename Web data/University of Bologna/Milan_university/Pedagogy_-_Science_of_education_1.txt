You will obtain a valid and, nowadays, necessary qualification to become a Pedagogist, a rapidly growing professional figure in both the public and private sector.

Experiences abroad
As part of its foreign experiences, the programme offers the opportunity to carry out internships abroad at international organisations and to participate in exchange programmes. These experiences allow direct contact with international concerns, enriching your understanding of the contexts and methods of care and education.
