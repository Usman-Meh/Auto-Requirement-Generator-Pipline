Experiences abroad
The programme offers the possibility of participating in internships and completing your thesis at international organisations and companies, as well as taking part in international mobility programmes.  These experiences will give you the opportunity to broaden your knowledge of the planning and management dynamics of agro-forestry and landscape in different environmental, production and economic-political contexts.
