Why choose this programme
A critical outlook and an innovative viewpoint on communication in all its forms.
The ability to analyse cultural objects and phenomena, as well as their structure, functions and meanings.
Creating and designing content for cultural products for media, publishing, new media and social media.
International perspective on relevant topics of contemporary debate: gender, conflict, climate change.
Multidisciplinary training facilitates entry into the world of job.
