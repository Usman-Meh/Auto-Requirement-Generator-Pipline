Why choose this programme
You will acquire skills and know-how enabling you to work in the research and dissemination of music and theatre.
You will acquire skills in the editing of texts for music and theatre, for performance purposes and for historical-critical investigations.
You will acquire soft skills that can be applied to designing and curating events in the performing arts.
You will acquire skills to coordinate audience education activities aimed at promoting knowledge of musical and theatrical languages.
