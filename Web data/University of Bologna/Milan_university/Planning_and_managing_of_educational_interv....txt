Why choose this programme
You will learn to design in the social field for different types of users and services, also in view of international calls for tenders
You will develop multidisciplinary professional knowledge and soft skills that are particularly valued in the world of work.
During your internship/thesis, you will apply your design knowledge in real-life contexts and experience the work environment.
You will experience interactive experiential learning techniques.
You will benefit from opportunities to connect with the world of work through the involvement of experts.

Experiences abroad
The programme supports international mobility by offering you the opportunity to spend a semester or an entire year in an EU or non-EU country, to spend a period with NGOs and other entities active in development cooperation, or to undertake an internship or thesis experience abroad aimed at studying a specific topic or developing career prospects.
