Why choose this programme
Because I value knowledge.
Because I want to develop critical competences.
Because I intend to develop a mindset capable of tackling and solving problems dynamically.
To acquire knowledge about the history and categories of Western thought, of different cultures and of science from antiquity to the present day.
To understand the critical areas of society: digital revolution, environmental crisis, forms of discrimination, scientific knowledge.
