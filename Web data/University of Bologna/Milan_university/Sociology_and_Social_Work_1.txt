Why choose this programme
You will acquire skills from a multidisciplinary perspective for the professions of Specialist Sociologist and Specialist Social Worker
You will acquire and consolidate skills in the critical analysis of emerging social phenomena and in the design of social policies and interventions
You will undertake multiple international experiences (double degree Bielefeld University, Erasmus, Overseas, thesis and internships abroad)
You will gain internship experience in a variety of institutions and organisations, where you will deal with several professional and research fields
You will become part of an inclusive community open to dialogue and exchange

Experiences abroad
The programme offers the opportunity to participate in study schemes abroad and to undertake internships in international organisations. The 'Sociology and Social Research' pathway leads to a double degree with Bielefeld University in Germany. The programme involves attending the first year in Bologna and the first semester of the second year in Bielefeld.
