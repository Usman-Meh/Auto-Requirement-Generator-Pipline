Why choose this programme
You will acquire skills and techniques in biological, chemical and engineering subjects for biotechnological applications
You will develop multidisciplinary skills to promote a more sustainable economy
You will take part in laboratory work in the various fields of biotechnology through an interconnected network of multidisciplinary labs
You will carry out basic, oriented or applied research for the development of molecular and industrial biotechnology
You will learn to address the latest challenges of biotechnology in the world of work with a view to improving quality of life and the environment
