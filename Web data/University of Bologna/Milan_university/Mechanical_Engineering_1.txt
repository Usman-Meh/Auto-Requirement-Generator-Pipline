Your training will be completed in close synergy with first-rate companies and research institutions.

Training is in line with the qualifications and professional profiles required by the labour market: choose your pathway
From theory to practice: Learning by Doing activities and Unibo Motorsport and Uniboat initiatives ensure challenging projects right from the start
Our students learn to translate ideas into actions and projects.

Experiences abroad
A wide range of exchange programmes are available to provide students with an international experience, both in Europe and in non-EU countries, including internships and thesis projects, with specific scholarships. A double degree agreement with the University of Duisburg-Essen (Germany) is in place for students in the field of 'Mechanics of Automation and Robotics'.
