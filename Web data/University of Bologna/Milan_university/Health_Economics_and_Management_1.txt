Why choose this programme
Acquire a solid knowledge of  health economics and policy, health care management, health law and regulation.
Joint diploma mutually acknowledged in four countries
Joint learning offer by four partner universities and joint activities with stakeholders and experts of the healthcare sector
Highly international and multidisciplinary environment with the opportunity of visiting up to 3 partner universities
Possibility to be granted an Erasmus Mundus scholarships
