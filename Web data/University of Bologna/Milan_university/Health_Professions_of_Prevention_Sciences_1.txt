Why choose this programme
You will learn more about the techniques acquired in the three-year programme, developing links between the different areas of prevention and health promotion.
You will acquire professional soft skills in the areas of management, training and research.
Through an internship, you will gain experience with prevention and resource management in national health organisations.
Employability is very high, over 95%, perfectly in line with the professional profile.
You will be able to participate in competitions for healthcare management positions, university teaching positions and PhDs.
