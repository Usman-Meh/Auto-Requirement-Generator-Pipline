Why choose this programme
The programme enables you to acquire professional and operational skills in all core civil engineering disciplines.
It makes room for autonomous activities aimed at conducting exercises and experimental workshops functional to the development of projects.
It allows you to carry out internships, also combined with your thesis, at international, national and regional companies.
It opens up professional opportunities in local or national bodies and in construction companies and professional firms.
It opens the door to professional and research pathways, such as Professional Master's Programmes (2nd Level) and PhDs.
