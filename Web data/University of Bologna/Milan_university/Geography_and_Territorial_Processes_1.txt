Why choose this programme
Prepare for a career in geography, or the teaching of geography in schools.
The programme teaches the cultural foundations and fundamentals of geographical disciplines.
It prepares students for a profession in cartography, including digital technologies.
Classroom lessons are complemented with workshops, internships, training abroad and field trips.
Study today's world, the environment, and processes in development and politics.
