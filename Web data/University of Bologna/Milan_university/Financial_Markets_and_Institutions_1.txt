Why choose this programme
You will acquire highly up-to-date skills and advanced knowledge of the financial sector.
Multidisciplinary training in a highly evolving and professionalised sector.
Classroom teaching promoted by testimonials from leading financial and corporate professionals.
You will have the opportunity to learn and apply methods-rules to financial organisations and contracts.
You will gain experience in consulting firms, financial intermediaries and companies.

Experiences abroad
The programme provides for Erasmus and Overseas exchange programmes, and offers the possibility of internships at international organisations.  It is also possible to prepare your thesis abroad. These experiences will put you directly in contact with international concerns, thereby enriching your skills.
