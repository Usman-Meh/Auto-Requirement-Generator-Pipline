Why choose this programme
Perform food risk analysis, prevention and management activities
Apply management and control systems aimed at guaranteeing the safety of food products
Be able to evaluate risk management and communication policies
Apply risk assessment and risk reduction strategies to reduce consumer exposure to known risks and possible emerging risks
Use the scientific method of investigation and apply knowledge and skills in solving problems in multiple areas of food safety
