You will be able to carry out internships in international companies, institutions and organisations and to participate in exchange programmes. These experiences allow direct contact with international concerns, enriching your understanding of global dynamics and providing a real advantage in terms of knowledge and in-depth analysis of the managerial job market in the sports sector.
