Why choose this programme
You will gain an all-round knowledge and insight into language sciences.
You will learn to describe and analyse linguistic data, considering different registers and varieties.
You will learn to apply statistical and computational methods to analyse linguistic data.
You will combine the study of language disciplines with soft skills such as teaching, interpersonal skills and leadership training.
You will participate in application-oriented workshops and specific internships designed to prepare you for the world of work.
