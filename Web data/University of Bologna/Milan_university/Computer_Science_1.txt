Why choose this programme
To specialise and have full control over advanced and innovative technologies, methods and tools in the field of information technology
To increase your skills and aspire to higher job positions, such as cloud analyst and designer
To complete the three-year programme, building on the sound foundations acquired in the previous cycle of studies
To acquire a flair for problem-solving and understanding innovative methodologies and technologies
To pursue a career as a researcher or researcher/developer in academia or in innovative software houses

Experiences abroad
As part of its foreign experiences, the programme offers the opportunity to carry out internships and exchange programmes, and to complete the final assessment (thesis) at international organisations. There is also the possibility of obtaining a double degree at Bielefeld University in Germany after spending a period of study there.
