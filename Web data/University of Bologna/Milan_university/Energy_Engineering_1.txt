Why choose this programme
Energy engineers play a key role in the ecological and energy transition as they shape the future of energy
Decide which type of energy engineer you want to become: an expert in energy efficiency and renewables, industrial energy or advanced technologies
Customise your CV with your choice of workshops, internships and electives
Practical training through educational workshops and internships in an academic or professional environment, in order to put the acquired knowledge into practice
Educational experiences abroad and at external institutions are favoured thanks to a 'white semester', i.e. a period with no compulsory courses

Experiences abroad
The programme allows you to acquire credits abroad through programmes, internships and theses, while also obtaining a financial contribution through participation in mobility programmes (Erasmus+, Overseas). Enhance your university experience and enrich your skills at leading international concerns such as the ITER Consortium, leading companies and top EU and non-EU universities
