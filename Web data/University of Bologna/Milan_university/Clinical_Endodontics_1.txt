Professional Profile
The Professional Master’s Programme aims to train master students into highly professional practitioners’ individuals in the following fields: endodontics, surgical endodontics, pediatric endodontics, microscope endodontics – in order to perform 360° endodontics both in the division and in your private practice. Master students acquire the necessary skills to carry out correct endodontic diagnosis and x-ray assessment, CBCT, manage complex endodontic cases using both rotary and reciprocating systems and the ability to treat surgical endodontic cases using the operating microscope. It is aimed to dentistry graduates and medicine graduates who wish to direct their practice in endodontics, primary teeth endodontics, surgical endodontics and implant rehabilitation.
