Why choose this programme
Insight into the most relevant topics for research in cognitive neuroscience.
Integration of basic theoretical and methodological knowledge with clinical case studies.
Practical laboratory work using key research techniques in neuropsychology and neuroscience.
Participation in seminars with internationally renowned researchers.
Opportunities for professional experience in the process of thesis preparation.
