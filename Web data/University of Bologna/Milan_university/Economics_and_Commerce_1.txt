Why choose this programme
This is a vocational programme, in light of employment data in companies, institutions and professional firms.
You will acquire a comprehensive, technical background in business, finance, trade and fiscal aspects.
You will find outstanding lecturers, many with a mixed baggage of research and teaching experience combined with a significant professional experience.
You will be able to get closer to the world of work through an extensive network of relationships with institutions, companies and professional firms.
The programme cooperates with the Order of Chartered Accountants and Auditors through an agreement that encourages registration.

Experiences abroad
The programme offers various types of experiences abroad, including internships at companies, world-wide exchange programmes, double degrees with 2 French business schools, and theses abroad. These experiences will not only enrich you personally and linguistically, but will also give you concrete competitive advantages in the labour market.
