Why choose this programme
You will continue your training in a multi-professional context.
You will undergo customised training.
You will be able to participate in and promote projects in Italy and abroad.
You will come into contact with a network of experienced professionals.
You will contribute to the development of the Nursing and Midwifery Sciences.

Experiences abroad
During the second year, there is the opportunity to enrich the programme with international experiences through internships at foreign partner universities, such as the Department of Nursing Science and Health Management at the University of Oulu in Finland. The duration varies from 30 days to six months, with partial financial support through the Erasmus schemes.
