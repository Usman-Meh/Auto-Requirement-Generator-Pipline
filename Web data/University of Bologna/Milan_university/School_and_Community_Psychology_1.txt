Why choose this programme
You will be able to orient your role as a psychologist toward areas other than the traditional ones and find new niche markets for your profession
The programme prepares you to use the skills of psychology to face the social challenges of a complex and ever-changing world
It includes on-the-job training to enhance your personal and professional skills (the tools you need in the labour market)
Schools need psychologists, and LAMSCOM offers you the training you need to work in schools
Learn to work with other professionals to promote the well-being of communities and individuals. Become a psychologist who makes a difference!
