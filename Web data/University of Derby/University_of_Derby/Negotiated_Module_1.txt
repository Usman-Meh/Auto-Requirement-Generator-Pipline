Module description
About this module
On this module you can propose, through a process of negotiation, an element of study equivalent to one module, or 20 credits, at undergraduate level 6.
You can study an area of interest to you, or your employer, that's not covered in the curriculum, or enhance the application of previous learning to your workplace.
Who is this course for
All health professionals.
Learning outcomes
Through discussion with your supervisor you will agree the appropriate learning outcomes and assessment method for your piece of work, and then complete and submit the work along with the learning contract.
