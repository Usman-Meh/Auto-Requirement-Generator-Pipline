Module description
About this module
This module reviews and critiques the main sources of research within cognitive behavioural theory and practice in order to establish a firm basis from which you can develop your use of evidence and knowledge to inform practice in this and future modules.
Who is this course for
Health & Social Care Professionals specifically those working within a mental health field.
Course team
Edith Moon
Dr Michael Townend
Learning outcomes
On completion of this course you should be able to:
Critically analyse, explore and describe cognitive behaviour theories and research evidence
Produce a cogent conceptualisation of a common presenting difficulty
Demonstrate the ability to plan and deliver interventions that take into account personal strengths and background factors.
Identify and critically evaluate the evidence base for the theory and practice of cognitive behaviour therapy
