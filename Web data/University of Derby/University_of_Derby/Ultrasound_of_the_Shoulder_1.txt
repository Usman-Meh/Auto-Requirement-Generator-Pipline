Module description
This interactive study day will combine a mixture of presentations, live demonstrations and hands-on practical scanning experience under the guidance of expert MSK Sonographers.
The topics which will be covered include anatomy, physiology, clinical assessment, normal and pathological ultrasound findings and reporting.
Who this course is for
Experienced ultrasound users or relevant point of care specialists
