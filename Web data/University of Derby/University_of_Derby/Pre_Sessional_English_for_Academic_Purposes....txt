Online course description
If you already have an offer to start a degree at the University of Derby, this course will give you an IELTS equivalent which will allow you to progress to the University of Derby programmes.
The course will help you:
Achieve the entry level for your degree course - either undergraduate or postgraduate
Improve your confidence before your degree course
Make friends and learn with fellow students who will be studying at Derby, before you begin your degree
This course is taught online and depending on your level of English on entry, you will need to study for 12, 8 or 5 weeks.
If you pass this Pre-sessional English course at the level stated in your degree offer, you will be able to automatically progress to your degree at the University of Derby. You will have achieved a minimum English level of CEFR B2.
You must be age 17 years or older to enrol on to this course.
Courses will only run if there is sufficient student demand.
Structure, certificates and assessment
This course will enable you to improve your skills and knowledge in the following areas:
Academic speaking, including presentations and seminar skills
Academic listening and note-taking
Academic reading, including research and referencing skills
Academic writing
Vocabulary
Grammar
Critical thinking skills
Understanding of British university culture
Please note that our modules are subject to change. We review the content of our courses regularly, making changes where necessary to improve your experience and graduate prospects.
How you will learn
This is a full-time course with 20 hours of study per week of which 12-15 hours of attendance of online classes are required alongside self-study. It is a highly interactive course, with lots of small group work and discussion. This will enable you to increase your confidence and learn from others.
