The JD program is not considered a graduate degree at UBC despite carrying Doctor in its name. Thus, the program is not administered by Graduate and Postdoctoral Studies. Admission to the JD program resides with the Peter A. Allard School of Law. Interested students should visit the
JD program website
for further information.
Overview
The Juris Doctor degree is granted on the successful completion of a three-year course and prepares students for admission to the practice of law and for business and public service.
For specific program requirements, please refer to the departmental program website
