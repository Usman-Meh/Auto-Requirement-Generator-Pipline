Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study hospitality, tourism and events Courses Postgraduate Diploma in International Hospitality Management Postgraduate Diploma in International Hospitality Management Advance your knowledge of hospitality in as little as one year, and study the Postgraduate Diploma in International Hospitality Management at AUT
If you have completed an undergraduate programme in hospitality and want to study more advanced courses in hospitality management, you can enrol in this one-year postgraduate diploma
The Postgraduate Diploma in International Hospitality Management also suits those already in the industry who want to enhance their skills and knowledge base at a more advanced level
