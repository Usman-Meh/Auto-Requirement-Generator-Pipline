Brochure Enquire How to apply Apply now Download programme guides Business (2024) Business (2025) What you study Career opportunities Related programmes International business and strategy focuses on the key skills to manage successfully in the global business environment, from working across different cultures and in diverse teams, to creating value through analytical and conceptual thinking, and managing innovation
Year 1 In the first semester, you take four core courses that cover fundamental business concepts: BUSS505 Consumer and Organisational Behaviour BUSS507 Financial Decision Making and Information Systems BUSS508 Economics and International Business BUSS509 Ethics, Responsibility and Sustainability In Semester 2, you study: INTB501 Business and Strategy in a Changing World You may also study another course related to international business and strategy, and also start to take the first courses from your second major, minor(s) or elective courses
Year 2 & 3 You gain an understanding of the international business environment, trade policies and internationalisation strategies, analyse business from cultural perspectives, engage in strategic planning and analytics, and examine disruption and innovation
International business and strategy courses may include: INTB603 Dynamic Environments INTB604 International Business Management INTB605 Business Strategy INTB706 Global Social Impact INTB707 Strategy in Uncertain Times INTB708 Innovation and Technology Strategy INTB709 Strategic Design You also continue to take courses from your second major, minor(s) or elective courses
