Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study business Courses Postgraduate Diploma in Business Postgraduate Diploma in Business With the Postgraduate Diploma in Business you develop advanced level business knowledge and skills in as little as a year
If you want to build on your undergraduate degree in business and gain expertise in contemporary business research, our Postgraduate Diploma in Business is ideal for you
This coursework-only programme covers advanced business concepts and research methodologies in depth
The Postgraduate Diploma in Business enhances your career prospects by equipping you with advanced skills that are highly valued in the business world
Graduates of this programme are also well-positioned to continue their studies in advanced degree programmes, for example the Master of Business or Master of Philosophy, paving the way for both academic and professional advancement
