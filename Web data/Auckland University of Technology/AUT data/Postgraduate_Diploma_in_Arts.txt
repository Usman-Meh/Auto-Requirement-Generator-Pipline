Courses Menu In this section Home Postgraduate Diploma in Arts Postgraduate Diploma in Arts In the Postgraduate Diploma in Arts you can choose from a wide range of courses, creating a very flexible course of study
In this one-year intensive programme you can select advanced level courses in culture and society you're interested in
You develop advanced knowledge in your chosen area, and further your critical thinking, problem-solving and communication skills
The Postgraduate Diploma in Arts also prepares you for further postgraduate study
