Courses Menu In this section Home Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of International Hospitality Management Conjoint Programmes Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of International Hospitality Management Conjoint Programmes Business acumen is invaluable in today's hospitality industry
The Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of International Hospitality Management conjoint programmes equip you with knowledge of both business and international hospitality management in as little as four years
You develop a solid understanding of the hospitality industry, as well as knowledge of core business disciplines and how they impact on each other
As a graduate of the Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of International Hospitality Management conjoint programmes you'll be prepared for a myriad of career opportunities across a number of different industries
