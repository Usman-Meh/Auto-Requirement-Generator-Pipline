For high-achieving students, the honours degree provides a fast-track to a postgraduate qualification that is highly valued by employers and a gateway to the PhD
Our programme emphasises modelling and data analytics for business and policy

Specialisation courses - complete the following ECON801 Microeconomic Theory and Policy (15 points)* ECON802 Macroeconomic Theory and Policy (15 points)* ECON803 Econometric Theory and Applications (15 points)* ECON808 International Trade Theory and Policy (15 points) *These courses are research methods courses in economics These advanced-level courses cover the latest research and theories in economics, and help you prepare for your independent research thesis or dissertation
Dissertation BUSS997 Dissertation (60 points) You can only enrol in the dissertation if you achieve a B- grade average or higher in your courses, have an approved research proposal, and supervision is available for your research
The class of honours awarded in the programme depends on your grade
