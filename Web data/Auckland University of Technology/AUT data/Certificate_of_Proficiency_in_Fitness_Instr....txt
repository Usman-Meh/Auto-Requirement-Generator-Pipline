Fitness instructing course overview The Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Instructing is the second of three courses you need to complete to become a qualified personal trainer and fitness instructor
This course focuses on the practical application of exercises, including cardiovascular, strength, and flexibility
You will develop applied skills in prescribing safe, effective and up-to-date exercise programmes
About this course Classes How to apply Related courses Topics include: Assessment of blood pressure, body composition, endurance, aerobic fitness, and muscular strength Exercise demonstration and spotting Programming for cardiovascular/aerobic fitness, fitness and weight loss Postural assessment and relative flexibility Resistance training programming for strength and hypertrophy When you have successfully completed this course you will receive a Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Instructing
Delivery mode Students are required to attend all online classes
Prerequisite Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations Who this fitness instructing course is suitable for If you’ve already completed the Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations and you’re ready for the next step in your journey to becoming a qualified personal trainer and fitness instructor, then this is the course for you
After completing this course The Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Instructing is the second of three courses you need to complete to become a qualified personal trainer and fitness instructor
The three courses are taught part-time over 4 months and each course is a prerequisite for the next course
When you've successfully completed the first course, the Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations (Level 4), you then enrol in this second course – the Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Instructing (Level 5)
This allows you to be recognised as a fitness instructor and become REPS registered nationally and internationally in the fitness industry
When you successfully complete the third and final course, the Certificate of Proficiency in Personal Training (Level 5), you will be qualified as a personal trainer and also be able to become REPS registered nationally and internationally in the fitness industry
