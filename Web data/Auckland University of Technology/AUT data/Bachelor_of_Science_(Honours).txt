The Bachelor of Science (Honours) is for students with an undergraduate degree in applied mathematics, computer science or science
At the heart of the programme is the honours dissertation; an independent small-scale research project on a scientific topic
We have strong links with key industry organisations, and many students complete their research project in collaboration with an industry partner
courses in the Bachelor of Science (Honours) focus on the latest developments in applied mathematics, computer science and science
