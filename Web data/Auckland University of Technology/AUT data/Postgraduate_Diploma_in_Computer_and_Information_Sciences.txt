You can choose from a wide range of courses – including artificial intelligence, big data, bioinformatics, 3D technologies and software architecture
Broaden your knowledge of specialist fields of computer and information sciences, and develop advanced analytical, planning and critical thinking skills
The programme can prepare you for further study in the Master of Computer and Information Sciences
