Courses Menu In this section Home Event Management Event Management Major - Bachelor of Arts A great event can generate positive publicity, create loyal clients and leave lasting memories
Event managers are the people behind these events
This major prepares you for a career in event management
As an event manager you plan, organise and manage events of all sizes – including concerts, conferences, weddings, award ceremonies and sports events
To work in event management you need an eye for detail and the ability to work across diverse areas including design, sound, project management, financial management, human resources, logistics, public relations and communication
Throughout your studies you plan events of various sizes and genres
You learn to prepare management plans, create contracts and write plans that help stakeholders evaluate their experience against pre-planned objectives
