Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study business Courses Postgraduate Certificate in Business Studies Postgraduate Certificate in Business Studies With the Postgraduate Certificate in Business Studies you can quickly upskill in areas of business that are of interest to you, change direction or contribute to your professional development
This programme can be tailored to suit your goals and can cover a range of subjects including finance, international business, marketing, accounting, business statistics, management and economics
Once you have completed this postgraduate certificate, you can consider further study in one of our postgraduate diploma or master’s programmes
