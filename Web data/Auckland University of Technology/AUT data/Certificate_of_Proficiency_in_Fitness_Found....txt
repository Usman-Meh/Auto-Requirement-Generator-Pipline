Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study sport and recreation Courses Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations The Fitness Foundations short course is designed to give you the scientific skills you need for a career in exercise, sport and fitness
You'll learn how to give an effective client pre-screening, fitness assessment, and exercise prescription
Fitness foundations course overview This is the first of three courses you need to complete to become a qualified fitness instructor and/or personal trainer
You can study all three courses at AUT
When you have successfully completed Fitness Foundations, you can progress to the Fitness Instructing short course and then the Personal Training short course
About this course Classes How to apply Related courses Topics include: Anatomy and muscle physiology Behaviour modification Biomechanical principles of training Cardiovascular and respiratory systems Components of fitness Customer service, health and safety Energy systems Lifespan Development and medical conditions Nutrition for fitness instructors Movement Competency Screening Training and weight training principles Endocrine system Delivery mode Students are required to attend all online classes
Who the fitness foundations course is suitable for If you’re interested in a career in exercise, sport or fitness – this is a great starting point
After completing this course The Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations is the first of three courses you need to complete to become a qualified personal trainer and fitness instructor
All three courses are taught part-time over 4 months and each course is a prerequisite for the next course
When you've successfully completed the first course, the Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Foundations (Level 4), you then enrol in our second course – the Certificate of Proficiency in Fitness Instructing (Level 5)
This enables you to be recognised as a fitness instructor and become REPS registered nationally and internationally in the fitness industry
When you successfully complete the third and final course, the Certificate of Proficiency in Personal Training (Level 5), you will be qualified as a personal trainer and also be able to become REPS registered nationally and internationally in the fitness industry
