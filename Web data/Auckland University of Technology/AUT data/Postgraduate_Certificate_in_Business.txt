Want a short postgraduate qualification that can prepare you for further studies and research? The Postgraduate Certificate in Business could be the right programme for you
By completing specialised coursework papers and advanced research methods courses you will develop advanced business subject knowledge and research skills
In as little as one semester, enhance your critical thinking abilities, analytical prowess and advanced problem-solving skills essential to thriving in today’s dynamic and complex business environment
The Postgraduate Certificate also serves as an ideal stepping stone for those aspiring to pursue the Master of Business research degree

Entry requirements What you study Student induction Related programmes Minimum entry requirements Completed one of the following or equivalent: Bachelor of Business OR Graduate Diploma in Business OR Relevant professional qualification approved by the Dean (or representative) to be equivalent to a bachelor’s degree Students who studied level 7 business courses as part of a degree different than stated above may also meet the entry requirements
If this applies to you, contact us to find out more
All final admission to this programme is subject to approval by the dean (or representative)
International student entry requirements English language requirements IELTS (Academic) 6.5 overall with a minimum score of 6.0 in each band English language requirements Contact us to find out more Faculty Postgraduate Research Office Email business.postgraduate@aut.ac.nz Phone +64 9 921 9999 ext 5280 To graduate with a Postgraduate Certificate in Business you must successfully complete 60 points from the Master of Business courses, subject to meeting prerequisite requirements
The Postgraduate Certificate in Business doesn’t include a research project, but can serve as a pathway into the Master of Business research degree
B- grade average in the Postgraduate Certificate is required to proceed to the MBus
