Food is more than nutrition
Food tells a story of time, place, culture, identity, and geo-politics
Food, its rituals and meanings help us to make sense of the world around us
Join us in the Master of Gastronomy as we discuss, debate and examine some of the most critical issues facing the future of our world
Eduniversal Group has ranked our Master of Gastronomy as the 17th best master's degree in the world for food and beverage management
