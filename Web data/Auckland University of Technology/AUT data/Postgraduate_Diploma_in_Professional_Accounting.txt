Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study business Courses Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting Further your understanding of accounting from a strategic business perspective in as little as eight months with the Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting
The Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting provides an entry point for students who don’t want to enrol in a full Master of Professional Accounting or who need added flexibility
All graduates from this postgraduate diploma are encouraged to continue into the Master of Professional Accounting
The programme is available as an exit qualification if required

To complete the requirements for the Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting, students must achieve 120 points from the Master of Professional Accounting
Complete the following courses ACCT860 Financial Accounting (15 points) ACCT861 Management Accounting for Business (15 points) ACCT862 Taxation in New Zealand (15 points) ACCT863 Accounting Information Systems (15 points) ACCT865 Advanced Financial Accounting (15 points) ACCT866 Strategic Management Accounting (15 points) ACCT867 Finance for Accountants (15 points) ACCT869 Accounting Regulations and Business Law (15 points) ECON863 Economics and Quantitative Methods (15 points) ACCT960 Auditing (15 points) Study planner for this programme Download our study planner to see what your studies will look like: 2025 study planner for the Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting Skills you will develop The outcomes for graduates of the Postgraduate Diploma in Professional Accounting are outlined in the Graduate Profile below
