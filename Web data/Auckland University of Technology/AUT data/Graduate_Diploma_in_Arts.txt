The one-year Graduate Diploma in Arts is ideal if you already have a degree
The Graduate Diploma in Arts is designed for students with an undergraduate degree who want to continue into further study and broaden their skills in as little as one year

Interpreters and translators can only sit a NAATI certification or credentialling test if they have completed a NAATI endorsed qualification
As a graduate of the Graduate Diploma in Arts in Interpreting you can sit the NAATI Certified Interpreter test
