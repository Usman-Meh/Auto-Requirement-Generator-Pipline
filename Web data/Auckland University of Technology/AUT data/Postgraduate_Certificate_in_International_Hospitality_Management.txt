Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study hospitality, tourism and events Courses Postgraduate Certificate in International Hospitality Management Postgraduate Certificate in International Hospitality Management This qualification is designed to meet the increasing demand from hospitality professionals for a postgraduate qualification with an international focus
If you who have completed an undergraduate programme in hospitality, and want to study more advanced courses in hospitality management, you can enrol in this half-year postgraduate certificate
The Postgraduate Certificate in International Hospitality Management also suits industry professionals who want to enhance their skills and knowledge
