Courses Menu In this section Home Postgraduate Diploma in Science Postgraduate Diploma in Science Advance your understanding of science, or computer and mathematical sciences in as little as one year with the Postgraduate Diploma in Science
This coursework-based qualification includes courses from the Master of Science
You can choose from a wide range of courses in science, or computer and mathematical sciences
Throughout your studies you develop advanced analytical, planning and critical thinking skills
Graduates may progress to further study in the Master of Science or Master of Science (Research)

Choose from a wide range of courses You can complete this postgraduate diploma without a specialisation, and choose from a wide range of courses in computer and mathematical sciences, or science
