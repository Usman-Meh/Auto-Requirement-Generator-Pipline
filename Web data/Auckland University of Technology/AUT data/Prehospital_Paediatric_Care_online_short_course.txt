Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study health sciences Courses Prehospital Paediatric Care online short course Prehospital Paediatric Care online short course This course is designed to refresh your knowledge and practice in paediatric care within the prehospital setting
It is run by our paramedicine team in conjunction with industry
Course overview This course includes online modules in the form of multimedia presentations (topics below)
Additional content in the form of articles, reviews, studies and multimedia videos are also provided to further enhance your learning and improve your practice
About this course Classes How to apply What you’ll study The multimedia presentations cover the following topics: General Considerations of Paediatric assessment Paediatric Respiratory Issues Paediatric Gastrointestinal Issues Paediatric Resuscitation and Cardiology Paediatric Haemodynamic Issues Paediatric Neurology Assessment Each online short course culminates in a compulsory formative MCQ quiz with a required end course pass mark of 80%
How long this course will take The course should take approximately 16 hours of online learning; however, you will have 6 months to complete the online learning
You will need to enrol to gain access to the online learning
Who this course is suitable for This course has been designed with working health professionals in mind
The pre-learning through the AUT online Canvas platform is designed to be a self-paced programme that you can progress through at your own speed, place and time; there is no onsite classroom requirements
This short course is primarily aimed at the prehospital emergency services including intensive care paramedics, paramedics and EMTs who have completed a Bachelor of Health Science in Paramedicine or those that have an Authority to Practice and want to remain current with their clinical practice
This course is open to domestic and international students
