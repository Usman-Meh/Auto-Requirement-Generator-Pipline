Courses Menu In this section Home New Zealand Sign Language and Deaf Studies New Zealand Sign Language and Deaf Studies Major - Bachelor of Arts New Zealand Sign Language is an official language of New Zealand, and an understanding of sign language and Deaf culture is useful for many careers
The New Zealand Sign Language and Deaf Studies major covers communication strategies in NZSL and the cultural knowledge to work with Deaf clients and colleagues
Sign language skills are in demand in all areas of New Zealand society, including education, health, legal and social service settings, as well as any customer-facing area of work
Tailor this sign language course to your interests, and combine it with courses from any other area of study, including education, health promotion or Māori development
