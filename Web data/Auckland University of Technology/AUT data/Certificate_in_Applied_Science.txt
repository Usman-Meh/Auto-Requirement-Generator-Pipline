Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Study science Courses Certificate in Applied Science Certificate in Applied Science Interested in science but not quite ready to start your university studies or not sure which direction to take? The Certificate in Applied Science gives you a taste of the many options in sciences or related areas
The Certificate in Applied Science is designed to help you develop the academic skills and confidence for study in a bachelor’s degree in science, medical laboratory science or health
Graduates of this certificate are also prepared for employment in entry-level positions in science-related industries, including laboratory assistant or trainee roles
