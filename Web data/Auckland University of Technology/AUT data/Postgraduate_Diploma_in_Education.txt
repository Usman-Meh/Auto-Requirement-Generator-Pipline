Study Menu In this section Home Study Study options Education Courses Postgraduate Diploma in Education Postgraduate Diploma in Education Gain advanced knowledge and critical analysis skills in the field of education with the one-year Postgraduate Diploma in Education
Through your studies you develop research skills, and explore different theoretical perspectives to apply across a wide range of contemporary issues and professional contexts in education
The programme caters for professionals from all education sectors and can prepare you for further study in the Master of Education

International student entry requirements English language requirements IELTS (Academic) 6.5 overall with 7.0 in Writing and all other bands 6.0 or higher; or equivalent English language requirements You need a total of 120 points to successfully complete the Postgraduate Diploma in Education
You can choose to take the Postgraduate Diploma in Education without a specialisation or specialise in educational leadership
To see the current timetable or for more details about the pathway you are interested in, please contact the postgraduate administrator at pg.education@aut.ac.nz If you want to specialise in educational leadership The Postgraduate Diploma in Education in Educational Leadership expands the knowledge and professional practice skills of current educational leaders and experienced educators aspiring to be leaders
