A sound understanding of computer and information sciences is essential for any business
With the Bachelor of Business and Bachelor of Computer and Information Sciences conjoint programmes you develop a sound technical understanding of computing and information technology and knowledge of core business disciplines
You gain the ability to plan, develop and apply appropriate technologies and tools to framing and solving problems in a range of contexts
