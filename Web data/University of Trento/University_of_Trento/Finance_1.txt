The course
The master's degree in
Finance
It provides an organic set of economic, management, quantitative and legal knowledge that constitute the reference framework to develop skills and knowledge in the perspective of economic research.The training course in the two-year period is articulated in a balanced way both by scientific-disciplinary areas and for themes and tools.
Training objectives
The master's degree course in Finance (degree class LM 16) has the aim of forming a multipurpose figure capable of operating in contact with increasingly complex and competitive financial markets, providing economic, management, quantitative and legal knowledge.
By applying the tools provided, the student will learn:
to read the evolution of the monetary, equity, debt and changes in the international macroeconomic context, also with statistical and ecconometric analysis tools;
to set coherent strategies of financial management, in the company, banking and financial investment;
To know and apply a wide range of investment tools, financing and management of the risk in a safe and appropriate way, according to recent techniques of financial engineering and risk management.
During the first year, skills in the economic area, corporate and mathematician/statistical area are acquired.During the second year they develop:
economic skills in the monetary and financial fields;
business skills in the field of risk management and company finance tools;
Econometric skills with specific applications for finance.
After graduation?
The professions that can give access to the master's degree in Finance are placeable both in the private and public sphere, with a mainly financial-generation or economic profile.
The typical entry location of the master's degree in Finance is in the position of operational or directive liability of the sector.In particular:
Analyst, trader, portfolio manager, risk manager at banks, savings management companies, securities intermediaries, insurance companies
Manager in the functions of financial management, treasury, risk management at industrial, commercial and services companies
Consultant at company of professional services and software oriented towards the financial environment
Economist at the offices Search for financial institutions, public and private research centers, statistics and central banks
actitor in insurance companies
