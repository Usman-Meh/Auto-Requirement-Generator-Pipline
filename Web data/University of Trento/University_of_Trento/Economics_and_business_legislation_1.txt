The course
The master's degree course in economics and business legislation is also proposed, also in function of the knowledge requirements requested at the entrance, to train masterly graduates ready to face the profession of
financial advisor
at the highest levels of qualification or in
Rules of responsibility in the Administration
of companies of all sizes.
The master's degree in economics and business legislation has been designed, in a coordinated way with the degree (three-year) in company administration and right in collaboration with all the professional orders of the Veneto, Trentino-Alto Adige/SÃ¼d Tirol and Friuli-Venezia Giulia, to prepare access to the profession of the accountant in the best way.
Training objectives
The master's degree course in Economics and Business Legislation (degree class LM-77) offers courses to acquire knowledge and application skills in the fields:
of the company administration, of the detection and accounting representation systems;
of the legal rules in the commercial field, work and bankruptcy procedures;
of the rules and the tax process also in a supranational perspective;
of the finance and financial management of companies;
extraordinary business operations;
revision and accounting certification.
After graduation?
The natural employment of graduates in economics and business legislation is the profession of the accountant independently or within professional consultancy companies and companies.The graduate also is equipped with excellent competences to take responsibility roles in the context of the administration of companies operating in a national and supranational dimension, and carry out activities in the field of accounting revision in companies in the sector.
The typical entry location of graduates in economics and business legislation is within:
professional studies of various dimension and articulation;
companies operating in the field of revision and accounting certification;
companies of all sizes, national and multinationals, with administrative roles;
non -profit organizations;
Bodies and public administrations.
