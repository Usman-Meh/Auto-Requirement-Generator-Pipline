Training objectives
The degree course in Civil Engineering forms a
professional figure able to operate within working groups dedicated to the design or implementation of engineering works
and that they are able to
acquire, even independently, further specific skills in the most application fields of civil engineering
.Further and important objective of the course of study is to provide a basic methodological preparation that allows graduates and graduates to take profit with the subsequent higher training courses.
The degree course in Civil Engineering allows you to also acquire the skills required by the construction sector now in the middle of the fourth industrial revolution.They mainly concern the
renewed relationship of civil works with the territory
and the
employment in the civil works of innovative smart and green technologies, of the Information Technology, and of the Internet of Things
.
What is studied
Professional profiles
Graduates and graduates in civil engineering will be able to find employment at
Professional studies and design companies of works, plants and infrastructures, in construction companies, public bodies and offices of design, planning, management and control of the building heritage, urban and territorial systems, transport systems
.Other professional opportunities may concern the
Directive careers or other roles of responsibility
In private and medium -sized private companies in buildings (construction), in transport and communications.
The functions that can perform graduates and graduates are mainly those of:
designer of works, plants and civil infrastructures (within the limits established for Junior engineers)
component of advanced design groups of civil works, systems and infrastructures
Responsible for planning, management and control of the building heritage, urban and territorial systems, transport systems
member of technical working groups in the field of Civil Protection and the territory
Testimonies on the professional prospects of graduates and graduates in civil engineering
Studies that can be taken after graduation
The preparation acquired with a degree in civil engineering can continue with the onsetting of the
Master's degree in Civil Engineering
.
In addition, the degree in Civil Engineering allows you to also access the master's degree courses in
Energy engineering
and in
Environmental Meteorology
Offered by the Department of Civil, Environmental and Mechanical Engineering.
