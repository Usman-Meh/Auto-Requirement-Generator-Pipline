Training objectives
The purpose of the degree course in mathematics of the University of Trento is the formation of graduates who:
have a solid knowledge of the basic notions and the methods of the various sectors of mathematics and in particular algebra, geometry, mathematical analysis and probability
demonstrate mathematical skills in reasoning, manipulation and calculation, and ability to build rigorous demonstrations
are capable of understanding and using mathematical descriptions and models in the field of physics, natural sciences, engineering, economy and human sciences;adequate IT skills
they have useful knowledge to critically reflect on mathematics and science, their methods and their development, and their relationship with human sciences and society
They are able to independently study mathematical texts, in Italian and in English, and to expose the material studied in writing and oral form, adequate to various types of public, using appropriate IT tools; they have adequate skills and tools for communication and managementinformation
They are capable of working in groups and inserting themselves promptly in the work environments; they have a mentality predisposed to the rapid learning of new concepts and methods.
Professional profiles
