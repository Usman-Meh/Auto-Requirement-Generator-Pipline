It is possible to access the master’s degree from a bachelor’s degree in Physics or any bachelor’s degree belonging to the study class L-30, in accordance with the annual regulations established for the application.
While students obtain the fundamental tools during the bachelor’s degree, studying the basic physics, the master’s degree deals with the current Physics main themes.
