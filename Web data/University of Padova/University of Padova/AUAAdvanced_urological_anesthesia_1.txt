AUA - Advanced urological anesthesia
Not activated for the a.y. 2024/2025
The
Master in Uro-Anaesthesiology
trains highly qualified professionals, capable of providing accurate indications to treat the main urological diseases in adult and paediatric patients.
The course provides a solid theoretical basis and essential hands-on skills to
integrate the specific knowledge and competences
of different disciplines, including urology, anaesthesiology, paediatrics and interventional radiology.
The final objective is to train specialists who can operate in a critical, independent and effective way in the field of urology-related anaesthesiology.
Formative content
The
Master in Uro-Anaesthesiology
helps students understand how to
manage anaesthesiology in urological patients,
for clinical procedures, endoscopic and endourological surgery, and in complex open surgery cases (e.g., complex blood clots). Moreover, it carefully assesses how to manage urological urgencies and emergencies, and how to deal with complications effectively.
The course includes
lesson cycles
concentrated in short periods, and agreed between professors and students, and care and/or observational activities at the University Hospital of Padua.
Students will get a chance to attend the Urology Unit, the Urology OR, X-Ray rooms, interventional X-Ray and reanimation rooms at the University Hospital of Padua. The course includes lessons and hands-on practice in the Urology OR, by witnessing urology surgeries, and during interventional X-Ray procedures.
Beneficiaries and employment opportunities
The
Master in Uro-Anaesthesiology
trains professional figures, such as Urologists and Anaesthesiologists.
Programme
The
Master in Uro-Anaesthesiology
provides specific training on the following topics:
Module 1 – Open urology surgery. The urologist’s message
Module 2 – Open urology surgery. The anaesthesiologist’s message
Module 3 – Robotic surgery. The urologist’s message.
Module 4 – Robotic urology surgery. The anaesthesiologist’s message.
Module 5 – Endoscopic surgery. The urologist’s message
Module 6 – Endoscopic surgery. The anaesthesiologist’s message
Module 7 – Outpatient urology surgery. The urologist’s message.
Module 8
–
Outpatient urology surgery. The anaesthesiologist’s message.
Module 9 – Surgical and conservative management of peri- and post-operative urology complications.
