The
Organizational Coaching: Leadership and Lifelong Learning
master provides comprehensive training on the principles and practices of organizational coaching and is designed for graduate students, managers, professionals and entrepreneurs who manage teams and work groups and feel the need to develop organizational contexts. Organizational coaching includes different levels such as: executive, team, leader. Organizational Coaching that promotes leadership and lifelong learning is focused on improving the behavior of people in organizations through learning, development and guidance. By adopting a participatory and engaging approach, which will characterize all the proposed activities, the Master will offer the opportunity to acquire specific credentials for professional curriculum. Practical activities will alternate with individual and group reflection.
Formative content
The
Organizational Coaching: Leadership and Lifelong Learning
master aims to promote the development of specific professional coaching skills and the mastering of methods and practices of organizational coaching, by expanding the participants’ experience and knowledge and by analyzing training and/or work challenges. Special attention is given to action research methods, how to give feedback and evaluation, and metacognitive aspects of conducting coaching with clients/coaches. The teaching format is distance learning but with different characteristics: the first and the last lessons adopt a hybrid mode, the others a synchronous and/or asynchronous mode.
The Master includes synchronous exercises on coaching practices and the completion of a project. The course ends with a final exam.
Beneficiaries and employment opportunities
The Master in
Organizational Coaching: Leadership and Lifelong Learning
opens up several career opportunities, especially in the field of professional coaching, a growing field that focuses on helping individuals and organizations achieve challenging goals and realize their full potential. Several professional coaching profiles are developed within the master’s program: the executive coach, who works with business leaders and managers to improve decision-making and performance; the career coach, who helps clients identify opportunities and strategies for professional success; the leadership coach, who works with leaders to develop and improve their effectiveness; the team coach, who improves group dynamics, collaboration, and productivity within organizations; and the business coach, who works with entrepreneurs to develop and grow their businesses, providing guidance on strategy, problem solving, and goal achievement. In organizations, coaching skills are now essential for human resource management, particularly for: facilitating learning and growth; developing talent and potential; addressing challenges and overcoming critical issues; and facilitating and supporting change and innovative progress.
Programme
The Master Organizational Coaching: Leadership and Lifelong Learning includes the following teachings and the implementation of the related modules:
Teaching: Fundamentals of Coaching
Modules:
Theoretical approaches and models of coaching
Professional implications and ethics in coaching
Contents:
Theory, research and practice of coaching in organizations; related ethical implications.
Teaching: Adult education
Module:
Lifelong learning and continuing education
Contents:
Theories and models of lifelong learning and applications in organizational contexts
Teaching: Organizational Leadership
Modules:
Coaching for leadership development
Coaching for transformative leadership
Contents:
Leadership theories and models and applications in personal and professional life.
Teaching: Career Development
Modules:
Professional development to Coaching individuals
Professional development to coaching teams
The role of coaching to address career issues
Reflective practices for personal career development
Contents:
Traditional and Contemporary Adult Career Development; diversity and inclusion, technology and career assessments, and organizational issues.
Teaching: Diversity, equity and inclusion
Modules:
Differences among groups
Promoting diversity values in organizational contexts
Content:
Coaching in dyads with diversity (e.g., age, class, race, gender, and other identities and positionalities)
Teaching: Personnel psychology and diversity
Modules:
Introduction to Personnel Psychology
Cross-cultural Issues in recruitment, selection and performance Management
Contents:
Theoretical foundations and key practices in human resources management. Diversity and performance management.
Teaching: Coaching methods and practices
Modules:
Developing and using coaching toolbox
Methods and practices of organizational coaching
Inventions applied in organizations
Contents:
Methods and practices of organizational coaching. Management of the coaching process, the use of evaluations, feedback, selection of interventions.
Teaching: Development of communities of practice in organizations
Modules:
Processes and strategies for developing communities of practices
Configuring a set of tools for developing community of practices
Activities oriented to developing practices for communities
Contents:
Communities of practice and social construction of knowledge within organizational systems. Models and strategies for building effective operational and organizational communities inspired by the principles of coaching.
Teaching: Coaching for teams and groups
Modules:
Activities to develop the practices of coaching teams
The use of technology for team and group coaching
Facilitating team and group coaching
Contents:
Coaching strategies for leading and developing teams and groups in organizations and communities.

FAQ
What are the characteristics of Distance Learning?
Synchronous distance learning involves interactive activities and practical exercises. Asynchronous teaching involves self-study materials, audio-video and short individual or group exercises.
What does project work involve?
Project work is built on authentic coaching with a real client in a real organizational setting.
What is the percentage of compulsory attendance and activities?
The percentage of compulsory attendance and activities is 70%.
